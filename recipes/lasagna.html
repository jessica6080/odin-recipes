Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

Below are the ingredients needed for this lasagna recipe:

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

Here's a step-by-step recipe to make some tasty lasagna!

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna with whatever ingredients you'd like
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
