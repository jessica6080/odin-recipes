Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound loaf frozen bread dough, thawed
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon water, or as needed
  • 1/3 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2/3 cup sifted confectioners' sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 dash vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Lightly grease 2 round cake pans with butter
  2. Roll bread dough out to rectangle. Brush with melted butter. Combine said ingredients. Cut log into 20 slices and arrange.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Pour heavy cream over dough
  4. Bake in preheated oven until golden brown, about 25 minutes
  5. Mix confectioners' sugar, milk, and vanilla extract in a small bowl; drizzle over warm cinnamon rolls to serve
